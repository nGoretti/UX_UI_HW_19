UX Skills

User Research

As a UX researcher, I employ a systematic approach using thorough interviews and usabiltiy testing, and utilizing tools such as user analytics software and prototyping tools, while relying on skills such as empathy, communication, and analysis to uncover user needs and behaviors.

User Empathy

In my approach to user empathy, I employ active listening and visualization tools like personas and empathy maps to deeply understand users' perspectives and needs, while honing skills such as emotional intelligence and cultural sensitivity.

Qualitative Interviews

I foster an open and comfortable environment, using open-ended questions and active listening to gather comprehensive insights from participants. For example, during a travel planning app interview, I probed the participant to uncover their preferences, pain points, and desired features, providing valuable input for design and functionality improvements.
